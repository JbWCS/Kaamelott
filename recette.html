Recipe / ingredients

It is necessary :
- 20 kg of cement
- 50kg of sand
Mix it all up! If you are making the Dame Seli tart, start by placing the gravel in a large container. Pour over the amount of sand provided, then the cement. As a reminder, if you are preparing mortar, do not put gravel! Mix everything well with a shovel. The mixture should be uniform and the cement well distributed. If you are using a waste bin, to Homogenize well you can make a heap with the mixture and move it 2 or 3 times.
